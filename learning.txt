**基本作用**：概率在深度学习中至关重要，影响从神经网络输出到数据分布的所有方面。

4. Basic Concepts
基本概念

5. **Probability**: A measure between 0 and 1 indicating the likelihood of an event.
**概率**：介于0和1之间的度量，表示事件发生的可能性。

6. Example: Probability of rain (0.25 or 25%).
示例：下雨的概率（0.25或25%）。

7. **Random Variable**: A variable representing possible outcomes of an event.
**随机变量**：表示事件可能结果的变量。

8. Discrete: E.g., outcome of a coin flip (heads or tails).
离散型：例如，掷硬币的结果（正面或反面）。

9. Continuous: E.g., values within a range [0, 1).
连续型：例如，[0, 1)范围内的值。

10. Sample Space and Events
样本空间和事件

11. **Sample Space**: The set of all possible outcomes.
**样本空间**：所有可能结果的集合。

12. Example: Coin flip {H, T}; Die roll {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}.
示例：掷硬币 {H, T}；掷骰子 {1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}。

13. **Events**: Outcomes of a process (e.g., rolling a four).
**事件**：一个过程的结果（例如，掷出四点）。

14. Probability Rules
概率规则

15. **Sum Rule**: For mutually exclusive events, the probability of either event occurring is the sum of their probabilities.
**和规则**：对于互斥事件，任一事件发生的概率是它们各自概率的总和。

16. Example: Rolling a four or five on a die.
示例：掷骰子掷出四点或五点。

17. **Product Rule**: For independent events, the probability of both events occurring is the product of their probabilities.
**积规则**：对于独立事件，两者都发生的概率是它们各自概率的乘积。

18. Example: Probability of being female and having brown eyes.
示例：既是女性又有棕色眼睛的概率。

1. Conditional Probability
条件概率

2. **Definition**: Probability of an event given another event has occurred.
**定义**：在另一事件已发生的情况下某事件发生的概率。

3. Example: Probability of picking a second red marble given the first was red.
示例：在第一次抽到红色弹珠的情况下再抽到红色弹珠的概率。

4. Joint and Marginal Probability
联合概率和边缘概率

5. **Joint Probability**: Probability of two events occurring together.
**联合概率**：两个事件同时发生的概率。

6. Example: Probability of being male and color-blind.
示例：既是男性又是色盲的概率。

7. **Marginal Probability**: Probability of an event irrespective of other variables.
**边缘概率**：不考虑其他变量情况下某事件发生的概率。

8. Example: Probability of being color-blind.
示例：色盲的概率。

9. Key Examples Demonstrating Probability Concepts
展示概率概念的关键示例

10. **Monty Hall Dilemma**: Illustrates conditional probability and the advantage of switching choices to increase winning probability.
**蒙提霍尔困境**：说明了条件概率和通过改变选择增加获胜概率的优势。

11. **Cancer Screening Example**: Shows how conditional probabilities can be misinterpreted and highlights the importance of Bayes’ theorem for accurate calculations.
**癌症筛查示例**：展示了条件概率如何被误解，并强调了贝叶斯定理在准确计算中的重要性。

概率的实际应用

15. Rules of Probability Explained with Examples
通过示例解释概率规则

16. **Probability of an Event**: Calculations using sample space.
**事件的概率**：使用样本空间进行计算。

17. Example: Rolling a sum with two dice.
示例：用两个骰子掷出一个总和。

18. **Complement of an Event**: Probability of an event not occurring.
**事件的补集**：事件不发生的概率。

19. Example: Not rolling a four on a die.
示例：掷骰子不掷出四点的概率。

高级主题

22. **Chain Rule for Probability**: Breaking down joint probabilities into conditional probabilities for multiple random variables.
**概率的链式法则**：将联合概率分解为多个随机变量的条件概率。

23. Example: Calculating the probability of selecting three people who haven't been to Boston in the fall.
